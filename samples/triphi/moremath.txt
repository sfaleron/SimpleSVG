r^2 = 2b^2 + 2a^2
x = r*cos(t)
y = r*sin(t)

x,y are known:

cos(t) = x/r
       = sqrt(3+sqrt(5))/sqrt(2+2*phi^2)
       = sqrt((3+sqrt(5))/(2+2*phi^2))
       = sqrt((3+sqrt(5))/(5+sqrt(5)))

sin(t) = y/r
       = sqrt(2/(5+sqrt(5)))
       = sqrt(1/2-sqrt(5)/10)
       = sqrt((5-sqrt(5))/10)

tan(t) = y/x = 1/phi = phi-1 = (sqrt(5)-1)/2
